## Wenchang Town

Wenchang 文昌 is located in the north-east of Hainan, about 65km south-east of Haikou. Wenchang is the capital of the Wenchang County.


**Wenchang Confucius Temple**

Chinese: 孔庙; pinyin: Kǒng miào

600 years old.
